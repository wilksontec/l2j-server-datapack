Newbie Guide:
At this level, if you prefer to use a specific attack skill, train hard to enhance that skill. Upgrade the ones you already use most when you become level 10.
As a rule, novices acquire an attack skill first and only later learn support skills. Therefore, it may be helpful to learn passive skills like Weapon Mastery, Armor Mastery, Magic Immunity or Soul Mastery. I also recommend that you learn Increased Power, which temporarily enhances your attack power.

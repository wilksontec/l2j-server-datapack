[Management of Clan Hall Devices]
NameExp Recovery Device
Cost
UseWhen clan members are resurrected in the clan hall, their Exp will be restored.

In order to use this facility, the fee must be paid in advance.
The first usage fee is paid from the clan lord's inventory. Usage fees incurred after that will be automatically withdrawn from the clan warehouse.
If the clan warehouse doesn't have sufficient Adena to pay the fee, the device will be reset.
